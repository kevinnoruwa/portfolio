Kevin Noruwa

Web Developer

I was always the most tech savvy growing up in the house ( at least in my head I actually belived this). My parents loved to call on me for help when it came to computers because I used to call my self the "technician" in the house. In reality I was no technician I just was good when it came down to basic computer stuff. I took compuer science in highschool as my elective thought it would be cool, but it was not as good as I Imangined in my head. Computer games all day, no home work, and no test is more of what I was dreaming for, instead we got a bunch of excel projects. Once I got to college I realized I had this creative side to me, I loved making videos & editing and just creating things and thinking of ideas from scratch. I was orginally going to school for physical therapy, a job that I would have not enjoyed because of the slow pace and long boring hours. I stumbled upon code on youtube, and as soon as I saw the whole concept of code and creating things from scratch using the computer I fell in love with it. I switched my path, I knew in an instant this is what I was meant to do for the rest of my life. Create things

-the Unknown developer
